* **Chunking**: Strategies for breaking long documents into digestible pieces.
* **Embedding**: Turning text into high-dimensional numerical vectors using models like Sentence Transformers.
* **Retrieval**: Finding relevant context using Similarity Search (Cosine Similarity).
* **Generation**: Passing retrieved context into the LLM prompt.
